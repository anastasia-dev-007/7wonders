Description

Great Wall of China

A massive fortification built along China's historical northern borders to protect against invasions.

Petra

An ancient city carved into the rose-red cliffs, known for its impressive rock-cut architecture and water management.

Christ the Redeemer

An iconic statue of Jesus Christ standing atop the Corcovado mountain, symbolizing Brazil's Christian heritage.

Machu Picchu

A well-preserved Incan city nestled in the Andes, famous for its remarkable stonework and scenic mountain views.

Chichen Itza

A complex of Mayan ruins featuring the towering El Castillo pyramid and various other temples and structures.

Colosseum

An amphitheater renowned for its gladiatorial contests and spectacles, representing the grandeur of ancient Rome.

Taj Mahal

A white marble mausoleum built by Emperor Shah Jahan as a tribute to his beloved wife, Mumtaz Mahal.
